Free Education Project

Preject Area:

Sirajgonj, Bhuiagati, Nimgachi, Bangladesh.

Preject Duration:

August 2014 to August 2019. Five Year’s Support Plan.

Objective:

We helped 30 school children of Class 3, Class 4 and Class 5 for five years. They Were provided all their educational needs with some extra needs like cloths, toys, sports things and awareness building leaflet, posters and books for their families. We are trying to help their families to be self-dependent and help to create income generating schemes.

Children Helped

30 school children from an ethnic community named (MAHATO) were directly involved with this support scheme. Furthermore their families were included in the long term development program.

Program Plan:

  • One of our representatives will be continually there for observing the progress and taking different actions directed by the directors.
  • One pastor and his wife will be involved to communicate the children’s families.
  • The country director will visit 30 families and children once a month to see the progress and direct the actions to be taken all around the month
  • Each children will be provided half of their school fees, necessary books, papers, pens, pencils and everything related to their education.
  • One expert teacher will go with director to teach the children and stay there for two days and teach them the update educational system.
  • One college student will be continually there to do tuition for children 4 days a week. He will be supported a fixed allowance for his voluntary work for children, and this support will directly go for his own college expenses and educational needs.
