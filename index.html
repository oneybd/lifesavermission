Our Vision

We desire as a Christian Community, to provide a Christ centred organization in Bangladesh. Our mission is to serve and minister to the needy and poor people, mostly the children and women in the Bangladesh provinces.

Main Aspire

The Lifesaver Mission Bangladesh desires to work in cooperation with others who share our aims and objectives of helping others, and to gain the support of both individuals and organization to achieve Matthew 25: 34-40.

Our Aim

As an organization, the long term aims of the lifesaver mission Bangladesh are twofold: Firstly, to help distraught people in Bangladesh. Secondly, to demonstrate our Christian faith by practical Christian service to the ones in most dire need.
